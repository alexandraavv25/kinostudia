Manifesto

KINOSTUDIAAA* is a women-led film studio developing narrative and documentary productions with a focus on politically and socially repressed communities.

The name is intentional. Drawn from the Russian kinostudia (film studio), the feminine grammatical ending signals what the studio stands for: a space built by women, for stories that have been consistently underfunded, undervalued, and underheard. The three A's represent insistence — we are here, we exist.

KINOSTUDIAAA* operates at the intersection of creative production and cultural strategy. It develops original narrative and documentary productions and positions them for international visibility through narrative development, strategic partnerships, and cultural programming. Its projects are grounded in feminist authorship, emotional rigour, and a commitment to narrative ownership: the belief that communities should control the stories told about them and profit from the value those stories create.
